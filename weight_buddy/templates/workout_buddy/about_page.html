About Gym Buddy

We're a small focused on providing tools and support for people who wants to take control over thier trainings. Given the saturation of information in the workout and diet industry, we focus on more pragmatic elements of exercising such as planning, setting up goals and tracking progress.

This site is the first part of our ongoing project to make effective workout plans and exercises guides available to everyone, especially people who are too busy to get stated on their own.
